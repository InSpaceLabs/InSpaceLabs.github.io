Increasing needs of the customers, rapidly changing technology and hence market, cost and time of design changes at the later stages of the design, differences between design and manufacturing capabilities puts enormous stress on the design team. Increasing population and needs of the people require highly efficient systems in short period of time. The only way to channel this load is to streamline the design and manufacturing processes. Systems engineering is the way to do that streamlining. Bell labs invented the systems engineering in 1940 and NASA perfected the method during moon mission. Now it is widely used for all complicated multidisciplinary projects. Now it is high time that we start using systems engineering process in all type of projects and businesses and reduce the cost and time to the customer. 


InSpace Labs has gain expertise with theoretical as well as practical experience, in exactly the same areas over the years. We would like your business to use this experience at the minimal cost so that you can save yourself and your customers lot of time and hence cost. Let us be the part of the process which you started, of changing the country and hence this world. Please leave a message with us and we will contact you back with the ways in which we can help you.

  • Mission Statement or need statement design and analysis
  • Stakeholder listing and Requirements Capture and Analysis
  • Breakdown of system into small parts and generation of alternative solutions for the same
  • Design phases of the project and its structure of activities
